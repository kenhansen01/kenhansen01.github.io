This is the icing on top. Ok, it's just a jekyll theme and its a work in progress.

## Some quick credits.
This theme borrows heavily from [Minimal Mistakes](https://mmistakes.github.io/minimal-mistakes/). It is an amazing piece of work.

## Why?
I am working my way through and changing all the floats to grid layouts (mainly for giggles), this should allow me to slim down the codebase a little bit. I am also changing / adding flavors (skins/themes) and changing file names to be more delicious.

This site is using this theme! All my sites are! I do change the flavors...
